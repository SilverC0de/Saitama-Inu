WILLIE D JOINS SAITAMA LLC CRYPTO AND NFT CONTENT CREATION TEAM IN MULTI-MILLION DOLLAR DEAL

LAWRENCE, Massachusetts (October 12, 2021) -- Saitama, one of the fastest growing cryptocurrency and community driven platforms has named Willie D as President of NFT Curation and Talent Acquisition in a deal reportedly worth over $50 Million with incentives and bonuses. 


Willie D, given name Willie Dennis is best known for his role as a member of the legendary rap group, Geto Boys. Willie D also has penned a top-rated advice column for the Houston Press, Vice Magazine, and currently host’s the popular Willie D Live Podcast on YouTube that boasts nearly 700,000 subscribers. In 2020, he and fellow Geto Boys bandmate Scarface, inked a deal with Charlamagne Tha God’s Black Effect Podcast Network and iHeart Podcasts to produce a 1-hour interview formatted show with celebrity, and newsmakers. On July 05, 2021, the Geto Boys Reloaded Podcast based in Houston, debuted at #3, and moved to #1 the following day on Apple Podcast/Music charts.


In his talent acquisition role, Willie D will be responsible for securing high level partnerships within several industries including, but not limited to, high profile individuals and businesses in the entertainment space. 


Similarly, Willie D will support Saitama in the creation of new NFTs. NFT stands for non-fungible token, a unique piece of digital art representing items such as photos, videos, audio, and other types of digital files.   


Willie D joins the Saitama team with over 6 years of cryptocurrency experience. He was an early investor in Dogecoin, and got in on Bitcoin before it exploded.


“Willie D is well respected in the entertainment industry and will bring a unique dynamic to Saitama and its vision”, says Russel Armand, Chief Operating Officer of Saitama. 


Willie D will attend the anticipated launch of Saitama’s mobile application, SaitaMask, next month at Hakkasan in Las Vegas, Nevada. 


Saitama LLC is a community driven platform that empowers people of all ages and cultures to be in control of their money and create their own wealth opportunities.
